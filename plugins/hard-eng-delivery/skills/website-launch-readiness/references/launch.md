# Launch readiness

Confirm the target environment, domain, hosting/CDN, release candidate, owner,
analytics consent model, indexing intent, and rollback. Check current official
requirements for DNS/TLS, platform deployment, robots/sitemaps/canonicals,
structured metadata, social previews, security headers and `security.txt`,
privacy/consent, accessibility, performance budgets, monitoring, and AI-agent
discovery where applicable.

Use real deployed read-only evidence first. Never change DNS, production
settings, analytics, redirects, or indexing without approval. Record exact
gaps, owner, proof, and rollback; do not claim launch-ready while a blocking
external check is unknown.
