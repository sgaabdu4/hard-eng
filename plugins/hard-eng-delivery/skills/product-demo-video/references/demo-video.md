# Product demo video

Use approved real-app footage or deterministic captures with sanitized data.
Define the narrative beats and acceptance before production. Reuse project
brand assets and motion owners; do not fabricate product behavior, customers,
metrics, or testimonials.

Keep raw/private recordings outside Git unless explicitly approved. Verify
legibility, crop, cursor/focus, captions, audio rights, duration, resolution,
and final playback. Publishing, sharing, narration generation, and external
uploads require their own approval. A marketing edit never replaces E2E proof.
