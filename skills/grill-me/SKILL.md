# Grill Me

Interview one clear, plain-language question at a time until the active stage is
aligned with no guesswork. Evidence-only: no evidence means `unknown`.

Read `references/start-routing.md` before choosing modules.
Before every user-facing interview question, read `modules/questions.md` in the
same turn and follow its Delivery contract; tool availability does not replace
the required visible question block.

During interview, visible reply is the next question only. Persist
`session_state.md` only after a session starts; it is the resume source of truth.
